The Turing Test

One of Turing's most famous contributions was the development of what became known as the "Turing Test." This test was designed to determine a machine's ability to exhibit intelligent behavior indistinguishable from that of a human.

How the Turing Test Works

The Turing Test involves a human evaluator who interacts with a machine and another human. If the evaluator cannot reliably distinguish the machine from the human, the machine is said to have passed the test.

ELIZA - Simulating a Rogerian Psychotherapist

Functionality

This is a simple simulation of the ELIZA program, which imitates a Rogerian psychotherapist. The application displays user messages and responds with pre-defined phrases encouraging the user to express themselves further.

Usage

1. Type a message in the input field.

2. Click the "Send" button to display your message.

3. After a short delay, ELIZA will respond with an encouraging phrase from its set of predefined responses.
